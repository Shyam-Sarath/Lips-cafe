# Product Requirements Document (PRD): Lips Cafe Website

**Project Name:** Lips Cafe Digital Storefront  
**Platform:** Responsive Web Landing Page (Mobile & Desktop)  
**Current Phase:** Prototyping & Design  

---

## 1. Objective & Vision
To create a vibrant, highly engaging single-page website for Lips Cafe that captures its unique "cozy, funky hideout" ambiance. The primary goal is to drive local foot traffic in Tondiarpet by showcasing their affordable comfort food, highlighting exceptional customer service, and providing essential visit information in a frictionless user experience.

---

## 2. Target Audience
* **Young Locals & Students:** Looking for an affordable, aesthetic place to hang out.
* **Families & Groups:** Seeking a comfortable space with enough room to relax and eat together.
* **Food Enthusiasts:** Searching for specific cravings like juicy fried chicken, burgers, and softy cones in the Tondiarpet area.

---

## 3. Key Features & Requirements

### A. Hero Section (First Impression)
* **Requirement:** High-impact visual header.
* **Content:** Large display typography featuring the business name and a welcoming tagline ("Your Cozy Little Hideout").
* **Media:** High-quality background image or video looping the cafe's neon interior and food.
* **Action:** Primary Call-To-Action (CTA) button linking to the Menu; Secondary CTA linking to Google Maps directions.

### B. "The Vibe" (About Us)
* **Requirement:** A brief text section establishing the brand's hospitality.
* **Content:** Focus on the customer service philosophy ("It's not just about the food, it's about how we treat and greet you").

### C. Interactive Digital Menu
* **Requirement:** A clean, grid-based display of the cafe's core offerings.
* **Categories:**
  * **The Sweet Spot:** Softy cones and ice creams.
  * **The Crunch Corner:** French fries and fried chicken.
  * **The Main Bites:** Burgers.
* **Details:** Must include item names, brief appetizing descriptions, and transparent pricing.

### D. Social Proof (Reviews)
* **Requirement:** A dedicated section building trust through community feedback.
* **Content:** A horizontal scrolling carousel or clean grid displaying 3–4 top reviews highlighting the ambiance, food quality, and welcoming staff.

### E. Footer & Utility
* **Requirement:** Critical business operations data.
* **Content:**
  * **Operating Hours:** 11:30 AM – 11:30 PM (Mon-Sun).
  * **Address:** 625, Thiruvottiyur High Rd, Tondiarpet, Chennai, Tamil Nadu 600081.
  * **Interactive Google Maps widget.**

---

## 4. Design & User Experience (UX)
* **Design Tooling:** UI/UX prototyping will be executed in Figma to establish the responsive layouts, typography hierarchy, and component variants prior to code generation.
* **Theme & Aesthetic:** A dark, moody background (deep slates/blacks) contrasted with vibrant, funky neon accents (pinks and purples) to match the physical location's lighting.
* **Typography:** Playful, thick display fonts for headers to match the cafe's fun energy, paired with clean sans-serif fonts for the menu items to ensure accessibility and readability.
* **Responsiveness:** Mobile-first approach. The layout must stack gracefully on small screens, as the primary audience will likely search for the cafe on their phones while on the go.

---

## 5. Technical Specifications
* **Prototyping/Generation:** Google Stitch (AI-assisted component and layout generation).
* **Frontend Framework:** React.js or HTML5/Tailwind CSS for lightweight, fast-loading performance.
* **Hosting:** Vercel, Netlify, or GitHub Pages (optimized for static/single-page sites).
* **Assets:** Actual user-uploaded photos from the cafe's Google Maps listing will be used instead of stock imagery to maintain authenticity.

---

## 6. Success Metrics
* **Performance:** Lighthouse score of 90+ for speed and accessibility.
* **Engagement:** High click-through rate (CTR) on the "Get Directions" and "Explore Menu" buttons.
* **Conversion:** Increased physical foot traffic and positive verbal/digital feedback referencing the website.
